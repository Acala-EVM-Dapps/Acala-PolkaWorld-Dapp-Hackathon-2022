MetaDefender is a special insurance project that can provide insurance for NFT contracts, chain game assets and even off-chain digital assets, as well as a standardized and scalable decentralized digital insurance agreement. Every market participant can freely insure or underwrite, and realize capital withdrawal at any time. The premium pool has a large underwriting capacity and changes in premium rates are more reasonable.
Based on community voting, we will introduce insurance for NFT star projects to provide security for users' on-chain game assets and off-chain digital assets.
We love to hear thoughts and questions from our community members. Our development team and community members are active on the following social media channels: Discord Twitter Telegram You can also find more information on the MetaDefender website.
MDF is currently not listed on a centralized exchange, but you can get it from a decentralized exchange on the mainnet that we have deployed.
Cover is available for purchase by users from the application interface using a Metamask account. 1) We are making the process as simple as possible. 2) Specify which smart contract address you want Cover for. 3) Specify the Cover Amount, currency and Cover Period. 4) Generate a quote and make the transaction using Metamask. 5) You are now covered!
Any premium pool allows anyone to provide their own funds and become an insurer. Select the projects you think are safe on the MetaDefender website and register MDF tokens as the underwriting capital. The underwriting capital has no lock-up period, and it can enter and exit freely.
After providing MDF tokens to the premium pool, the insurer can obtain an equivalent amount of sMDF as a capital certificate. sMDF can be sold at any time on decentralized exchanges and exchanged for MDF. The trading depth is provided and guaranteed by the official MetaDefender.
A token is needed in order to bind together the internal economics of the mutual, so that the platform can: Dynamically react to the prevailing conditions and capitalization levels; Reward active members for participating in the day-to-day operations of the mutual.
